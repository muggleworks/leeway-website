how it works!

Managing your vehicle's fuel expenses has never been easier. Simply add an entry every time you refuel with relevant information, and we'll take care of the rest. Our advanced processing algorithms will analyze the data and provide you with valuable insights on your vehicle's mileage and cost per kilometer. It's a hassle-free solution to keep track of your expenses and optimize your vehicle's performance.

faq’s

Are the insights accurate?

Yes, the insights provided by our vehicle expense tracker are accurate. Our advanced data processing algorithms analyze the information you provide, such as fuel expenses and mileage, to generate these insights. It's worth noting that the accuracy of the insights improves as you input more data into the system. Therefore, we recommend inputting your vehicle's expenses regularly and ensuring the information isaccurate and up-to-date to obtain the most precise insights possible.

Is it possible to track expenses for multiple vehicles?

At the moment, tracking expenses for multiple vehicles is not available, but we are constantly improving our product and adding new features to meet our users' needs. We are happy to inform you that the ability to track expenses for multiple vehicles is coming soon. Please stay tuned for updates and feel free to reach out to us if you have any further questions or feedback.

Is it necessary to create an account to use the app?

Currently, to use our app, you need to create an account using either your Google or Apple sign-in credentials. However, we are continuously improving our product and working on a new version that will allow users to use the app without the need for an account. We understand that this is an important feature for some of our users, and we're committed to making it happen. Stay tuned for updates on the new version,and feel free to contact us if you have any further questions or feedback.

Is my data stored securely with encryption?

At the moment, we don't have encryption for the stored data. However, we take data security and privacy very seriously, and we're actively working on adding encryption to our platform in the near future. We want to ensure that all our users' data is protected and secure.
